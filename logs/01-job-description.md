# Job Description: Engineering Manager

## Role / Profession

Engineering Manager at a SaaS-based billing solutions company, responsible for leading Development and QA teams.

---

## Daily Tasks

- Collaborate with Product to gather requirements and translate them into technical JIRA tickets
- Participate in meetings with Product, DevOps, Support, and Development teams
- Review technical designs and perform code reviews
- Coordinate with Support on customer issue escalations
- Work with DevOps on infrastructure, performance, and scaling needs
- Capture meeting outcomes, decisions, and action items from design and planning discussions

---

## Weekly Tasks

- Sprint planning, backlog grooming, and progress tracking
- Review and finalize meeting minutes and actionable items from cross-functional meetings
- Create or update technical design documentation based on design discussions
- Publish technical documentation to SharePoint or Confluence
- Report team progress and project updates to senior management

---

## Cognitive Load

- Translating business and meeting discussions into clear technical requirements
- Making architectural and prioritization decisions
- Reviewing code and design proposals
- Synthesizing meeting discussions into structured outcomes and documentation

---

## Repetitive Patterns

- Writing and refining JIRA tickets
- Reviewing pull requests
- Summarizing meetings into minutes and action items
- Updating technical documentation
- Status updates and stakeholder follow-ups

---

## Pain Points

- Frequent context switching across meetings, reviews, and escalations
- Manual effort required to document meetings and decisions
- Managing dependencies and unclear or evolving requirements
